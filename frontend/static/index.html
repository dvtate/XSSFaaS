XSSaaS

Yet another serverless FaaS platform except this one runs your functions in random people's browsers. Or alternatively you could self-host and use your own computer lab.

Technology In Use

XSSaaS uses and enables the use of modern web technology in order to maximize performance and ease of use while avoiding the bloat of many popular frameworks.

  • Web Workers
  • WebSockets
  • HTML + TypeScript + Webpack (no frameworks)
  • WebAssembly
